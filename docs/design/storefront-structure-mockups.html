Question 3B · new

The ticket chooser — two rows or one toggle?

The chooser's cardinality is fixed by the product: a series sells at most two tiers (Hele kurset + optional Drop-in), and a single course or workshop sells exactly one — the shipped rail already hides the selector entirely at one tier (tiles.length > 1 gate in BookingRailLite). That makes a segmented toggle a legitimate alternative to rows: it can never overflow. Both panels below are the same V1 booking panel — head, summary, CTA and edge-state rules identical — only the middle zone changes. Both are clickable.
